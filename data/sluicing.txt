# sluicing.txt
# Some examples by Van Holthenrichs & Maria Shardakova
# Some examples taken from Russian literature (Tolstoy & Dostoyevsky) and added by Van Holthenrichs
# language: rus
# Notes: Sluicing was by and far the easiest type of ellipsis to generate data for by drawing from existing literature. 
# These are all grammatical examples. The elided positions are indicated by ___.

Я говорю по-русски, но не очень хорошо ___.
----
Я говорю по-русски, но не очень хорошо говорю по-русски.
